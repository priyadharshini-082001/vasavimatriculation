who we are

Vasavi Vidhyalaya Matriculation School

Vasavi Vidhyalaya Matriculation School is a dedicated educational institution committed to providing quality education and shaping responsible future citizens. Located in Tiruchirappalli, the school focuses on nurturing young minds through a balanced approach that combines academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development. Our institution believes that education is not just about learning from books, but about building character, confidence, and leadership qualities in every student. With a team of passionate teachers and supportive staff, Vasavi Vidhyalaya provides a positive and inspiring learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their talents and reach their full potential. At Vasavi Vidhyalaya, we emphasize discipline, creativity, critical thinking, and innovation. Along with strong academic programs, the school promotes participation in cultural activities, sports, and co-curricular programs that help students grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally.

Academic Excellence

"Vasavi Vidhyalaya Matriculation School, Trichy has proudly achieved multiple national and international world records, including a prestigious Guinness World Record for creating the world’s longest continuous cartoon strip (601 metres) in 2010. The school has also been recognized by Elite World Records, Asian Records Academy, and Tamilan Book of Records for remarkable achievements such as the largest origami national flag, largest footprint painting by three generations, seed mosaic creation, and simultaneous paper arrow making The first School in India To Create Two Guiness World Record"
